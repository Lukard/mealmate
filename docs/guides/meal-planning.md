# Meal Planning Guide

This guide covers advanced meal planning features in Meal Automation.

## Understanding Your Meal Plan

### Plan Structure

Each weekly meal plan includes:

| Meal Type | Timing | Included By Default |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Breakfast | Morning | Yes |
| Lunch | Midday | Yes |
| Dinner | Evening | Yes |
| Snacks | Throughout day | Optional |

### Recipe Information

Each meal shows:
- Recipe name and image
- Preparation and cooking time
- Difficulty level
- Servings (adjusted to your household)
- Nutritional summary
- Main ingredients

## Customizing Your Plan

### Swapping Meals

1. Click on any meal you want to change
2. View alternative suggestions
3. Filter by:
   - Cooking time
   - Cuisine type
   - Ingredients available
   - Dietary requirements
4. Select your preferred alternative
5. The grocery list updates automatically

### Locking Meals

Found a meal you love? Lock it in place:

1. Click the lock icon on the meal
2. Locked meals won't change when regenerating
3. Great for weekly favorites

### Regenerating Plans

Need a fresh start?

1. Unlock any meals you don't want to keep
2. Click "Regenerate Plan"
3. Only unlocked meals will change
4. Your preferences are always respected

## Advanced Features

### Meal Planning Preferences

Fine-tune your plans in Settings:

**Variety Settings:**
- Maximum repetition per week (default: 2)
- Ingredient diversity level
- Cuisine rotation

**Time Constraints:**
- Weekday cooking time limit
- Weekend cooking time limit
- Quick meal minimum per week

**Budget Control:**
- Weekly budget target
- Cost-per-meal limits
- Prefer sale items

### Seasonal Awareness

Meal Automation suggests seasonal recipes:

| Season | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| Spring | Light salads, fresh vegetables |
| Summer | Cold soups, grilled dishes |
| Autumn | Stews, root vegetables |
| Winter | Hearty soups, comfort food |

## Saving and Reusing Plans

### Favorite Plans

Save a complete weekly plan:

1. Click "Save as Favorite"
2. Name your plan (e.g., "Summer Light Week")
3. Access from Favorites section
4. Apply to any future week

### Meal History

View and reuse past meals:

1. Go to History section
2. Browse by date or search
3. Click any meal to add to current plan
4. Track which meals were hits or misses

## Tips for Better Plans

### 1. Batch Cooking

Look for meals with shared ingredients:
- Make extra rice for multiple dishes
- Use chicken in consecutive meals
- Prep vegetables in advance

### 2. Leftover Planning

Some recipes generate planned leftovers:
- Sunday roast becomes Monday sandwiches
- Extra pasta sauce freezes for later
- Batch soups for quick lunches

### 3. Balance Nutrition

Ensure variety in:
- Protein sources (meat, fish, legumes)
- Vegetable colors (aim for rainbow)
- Whole grains vs. refined
- Cooking methods (not all fried!)

## Troubleshooting

### "Not enough variety"

- Increase your cuisine preferences
- Expand dietary restrictions if possible
- Enable more recipe categories

### "Meals too complex"

- Adjust skill level in settings
- Set stricter time limits
- Enable "Quick Meals Only" filter

### "Budget too high"

- Set budget limits in preferences
- Enable "Budget Mode"
- Prioritize store-brand products
