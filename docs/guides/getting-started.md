# Getting Started with Meal Automation

Welcome to Meal Automation! This guide will help you set up your account and create your first meal plan.

## Overview

Meal Automation helps you:
- Plan your weekly meals based on your preferences
- Generate grocery lists automatically
- Compare prices across Spanish supermarkets
- Automate your shopping with our browser extension

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Profile

When you first access Meal Automation, you'll be asked a few questions to personalize your experience:

1. **Household Information**
   - How many adults in your household?
   - How many children? (and their ages)

2. **Dietary Preferences**
   - Any food allergies or intolerances?
   - Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
   - Cuisine preferences (Spanish, Mediterranean, international)

3. **Budget & Shopping**
   - Weekly grocery budget
   - Preferred supermarkets (Mercadona, Carrefour, Lidl, etc.)
   - Delivery or pickup preference

4. **Cooking Preferences**
   - Available cooking time on weekdays vs. weekends
   - Skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
   - Kitchen equipment available

### Step 2: Generate Your First Meal Plan

Once your profile is complete:

1. Click **"Generate Meal Plan"**
2. Review your personalized weekly plan
3. Each day shows:
   - Breakfast
   - Lunch
   - Dinner
   - Snacks (optional)

### Step 3: Customize Your Plan

Don't like a suggested meal? No problem!

- Click on any meal to see alternatives
- Use the **"Swap"** button to replace it
- Click **"Lock"** on meals you want to keep
- Click **"Regenerate"** to refresh unlocked meals

### Step 4: Review Your Grocery List

Once you're happy with your meal plan:

1. Click **"View Grocery List"**
2. Review all ingredients needed for the week
3. Check off items you already have at home
4. Add any additional items manually

### Step 5: Shop Automatically

Ready to order? Use our browser extension:

1. Install the Meal Automation extension (see [Browser Extension Guide](./browser-extension.md))
2. Select your preferred supermarket
3. Click **"Send to Cart"**
4. The extension opens your supermarket's website
5. Products are automatically added to your cart
6. Review and complete your purchase

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Be Specific About Preferences
The more details you provide about your dietary needs and preferences, the better your meal plans will be.

### 2. Review and Adjust
Your first meal plan is a starting point. Use the swap feature to fine-tune recommendations.

### 3. Check Your Pantry
Before generating a list, mark items you already have to avoid over-buying.

### 4. Plan Ahead
Generate your meal plan early in the week to take advantage of sales and promotions.

### 5. Save Favorites
When you find a meal plan you love, save it as a favorite for quick reuse.

## Common Questions

### How do I change my dietary preferences?
Go to **Settings > Profile > Dietary Preferences** to update your restrictions and preferences.

### Can I plan for more than one week?
Currently, we generate weekly plans. You can regenerate each week or save plans for rotation.

### What if a product is out of stock?
Our extension will alert you and suggest similar alternatives available in the store.

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use encryption for all personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](../privacy.md) for details.
