# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about Meal Automation.

## General Questions

### What is Meal Automation?

Meal Automation is a meal planning and grocery shopping assistant designed for Spanish households. It helps you:
- Plan weekly meals based on your preferences
- Generate optimized grocery lists
- Compare prices across Spanish supermarkets
- Automate grocery shopping with our browser extension

### Is Meal Automation free?

We offer a free tier with basic features. Premium features are available through subscription. See our pricing page for details.

### Which supermarkets are supported?

Currently supported:
- Mercadona

Coming soon:
- Carrefour
- Lidl
- Dia
- Alcampo

See [Supermarket Integration](./supermarket-integration.md) for the full roadmap.

### Is Meal Automation available outside Spain?

Currently, Meal Automation is designed specifically for the Spanish market. We may expand to other countries in the future.

## Account & Privacy

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit our website or download the app
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Complete the initial preferences questionnaire
5. Start planning!

### What data do you collect?

We collect:
- Email and account information
- Meal planning preferences
- Grocery list history (to improve recommendations)
- Anonymous usage analytics

We never collect:
- Supermarket passwords
- Payment information
- Data you don't explicitly provide

### How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We use:
- HTTPS encryption for all communications
- Encrypted storage for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- GDPR-compliant data handling

## Meal Planning

### How does meal plan generation work?

Our algorithm considers:
- Your dietary restrictions and preferences
- Household size and ages
- Budget constraints
- Cooking time availability
- Seasonal ingredients
- Nutritional balance
- Recipe variety

### Can I customize my meal plan?

Absolutely! You can:
- Swap any meal for alternatives
- Lock meals you want to keep
- Add your own recipes
- Regenerate partially or fully
- Save favorite plans

### How many recipes do you have?

Our database includes 200+ curated recipes, focusing on:
- Traditional Spanish cuisine
- Mediterranean dishes
- Quick weekday meals
- Family-friendly options
- Budget-conscious recipes

### Can I add my own recipes?

Yes! Go to Recipes > Add Recipe to input your own recipes. They'll be included in future meal plan suggestions.

### Do you support special diets?

We support:
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Gluten-free
- Lactose-free
- Nut allergies
- Shellfish allergies
- Low-sodium
- Diabetic-friendly
- And more...

## Grocery Lists

### How are grocery lists generated?

From your meal plan:
1. Ingredients extracted from all recipes
2. Quantities calculated for your household size
3. Duplicates combined and optimized
4. Items matched to supermarket products
5. List organized by category

### Can I modify the grocery list?

Yes! You can:
- Add items manually
- Remove items
- Change quantities
- Mark items as "already have"
- Change product selections

### How accurate are the prices?

Prices are updated daily and typically within 5% of in-store prices. Some variations may occur due to:
- Regional differences
- Flash promotions
- Price changes during the day

### Can I share my grocery list?

Yes! You can:
- Share via link with family members
- Export to PDF for printing
- Sync across devices
- Send to connected household accounts

## Browser Extension

### Which browsers are supported?

Currently:
- Google Chrome (version 90+)

Coming soon:
- Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari

### Is the extension safe?

Yes. The extension:
- Only activates on supermarket websites
- Never stores your passwords
- Doesn't access payment information
- Uses secure communication
- Is regularly audited

### Why isn't a product being found?

Possible reasons:
- Product out of stock
- Regional availability
- Name variation in our database
- Recently added product not indexed

Solutions:
- Accept suggested alternatives
- Add manually on supermarket site
- Report missing product to help us improve

### The cart isn't filling - what do I do?

Try these steps:
1. Ensure you're logged into the supermarket
2. Disable ad blockers temporarily
3. Clear extension cache (Settings > Clear Data)
4. Update the extension
5. Try a smaller batch of items

## Technical Questions

### What are the system requirements?

**Web App:**
- Modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection

**Extension:**
- Google Chrome 90+
- Windows, Mac, or Linux

### Why is meal planning slow?

This could be due to:
- Large household size (more combinations)
- Many dietary restrictions
- Server load during peak hours
- Your internet connection

### Can I use Meal Automation offline?

Limited functionality is available offline:
- View saved meal plans
- Access downloaded recipes
- View cached grocery lists

You need internet for:
- Generating new plans
- Price updates
- Cart automation

### How do I report a bug?

1. Go to Settings > Help > Report Bug
2. Describe the issue
3. Include screenshots if possible
4. We'll investigate and respond

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login screen
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### My meal plan won't generate

Check that:
- You've completed the preferences questionnaire
- Your dietary restrictions aren't too restrictive
- You have internet connection
- Try refreshing and regenerating

### Products in my cart don't match my list

This can happen when:
- Exact product unavailable
- Size variation selected
- Algorithm chose best match

To fix:
- Review substitutions before checkout
- Adjust preferences for future orders
- Report mismatches for improvement

### The extension crashed

1. Refresh the supermarket page
2. Click extension icon to restart
3. Resume from where you left off
4. Report the crash for investigation
